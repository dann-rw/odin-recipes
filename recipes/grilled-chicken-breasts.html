Grilled Chicken Breasts

Description

These fresh, tender, and juicy chicken breasts are packed full of protein and ready to satisfy your hunger!

This chicken recipe is easy and versatile, where it is just as easy to prepare this dish for an individual or a group

Ingriedients

  • Skinless chicken breasts x4
  • 1/2 cup of lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon of onion powder
  • Ground black pepper
  • Salt
  • Dried parsley x2 teaspoons

How to make grilled chicken breasts

  1. Preheat oven to 200 degrees
  2. Dip chicken in lemon juice and evenly distribute the onion powder, ground black pepper, salt, and parsley over them
  3. Grill marinated chicken breasts until cooked and juices have run clear, 8-10 mins per side
  4. Serve!
