Stirfry

Description

This chicken and broccoli stir fry recipe is easy to make. All you need to do is stir-fry some chicken pieces with fresh broccoli in soy, ginger, garlic, and brown sugar. When finished, serve this with rice for a healthy and filling dinner.

This chicken and broccoli stir fry is a great option if you are in need of a quick and easy, yet healthy, dinner!

Ingriedients

  • Oil
  • Chicken
  • Onion
  • Ginger and Garlic
  • Broccoli
  • Water
  • Oyster sauce and Soy sauce
  • Brown sugar
  • Seasonings; crushed red pepper and black pepper

How to make chicken and broccoli stir fry

  1. Coat the Chicken in oil and seasoning
  2. Cook until crisp on all sides in hot oil
  3. Add the onions, ginger, and garlic to cook
  4. Transfer to a plate
  5. Cook the broccoli in the skillet
  6. Meanwhile, make the sauce by stirring the soy and oyster sauces together
  7. Add the Chicken to the broccoli then add the sauce
  8. Cook until the chicken and broccoli are well coated and the sauces is thickened
